The Kobeni Burger

Description

A classic family burger so good that it will have heads rolling! Think you won't be able to make the burger blessed by Fujimoto? Well think again, as the Kobeni Burger exceeds in its simplicity, allowing any family to enjoy it. Best enjoyed with a clumsy cute japanese waitress who used to be a public devil hunter.

"Best Vamvaya of my life! A great meal to enjoy with my date!" - Chainsawman 2019.

Ingredients

  • Tomato!
  • Lettuce!
  • Cheese!
  • Bun!
  • And the star that packs a punch is the... Beef!

Steps:

  1. Place two buns in toaster until toasted.
  2. Put two tomato slices in.
  3. Put lettuce and cheese.
  4. Place cooked beef on.
  5. Give to Kobeni to serve to your Family Burger Customers!
